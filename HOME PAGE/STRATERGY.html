TICKET-LESS ENTRY SYSTEM

DESCRIPTION

This stratergy is all about an app that helps our vacation more comfort and easier. This app simply allows us to book our tickets through an app for monuments or temples. This system would be great one for those who plans to visit more places in a shorter span without any dis-comfort. Not only this, but also the time for waiting to collect our tickets is getting reduced without waiting in a long queue.

FEATURES AND BENEFITS

  • Book your visits easily.
  • Visit more monuments in a short span.
  • More and More...

SPECIFICATIONS

  1. When you decide to visit monuments or temples just go to our website
  2. Login with your anyone of the identity proofs
  3. click on the state and the monuments you wish to visit
  4. Enter the required details field like Name,Age,number of people accompanying you etc.,
  5. Click on submit
  6. Your name will be in waiting list and once it is conformed you will receive an SMS with copies of tickes.
